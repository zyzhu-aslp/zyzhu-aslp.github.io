## 实验室简介

音频语音与语言处理实验室（ASLP@NPU）由西北工业大学谢磊教授领衔，是国内外语音、音频与语言智能领域具有广泛影响力的研究团队。

实验室围绕语音识别、语音合成、语音增强、口语对话系统以及新兴音频语言模型等方向开展前沿研究，始终坚持学术创新与实际应用并重。所建设的 **WeNet** 工具平台与 **WenetSpeech** 数据资源已被学术界和工业界广泛采用。

**链接：** [实验室主页](http://www.npu-aslp.org/) · [WeNet](https://github.com/wenet-e2e/wenet) · [WenetSpeech](https://github.com/wenet-e2e/WenetSpeech)

## Lab Overview

The Audio, Speech and Language Processing Lab (ASLP@NPU), led by Prof. Lei Xie at Northwestern Polytechnical University, is widely recognized as one of the leading research groups in speech, audio, and language technologies.

The lab conducts cutting-edge research spanning speech recognition, speech synthesis, speech enhancement, spoken dialogue systems, and emerging audio language models. Its open-source platforms — including **WeNet** and **WenetSpeech** — have been widely adopted by both academia and industry.
